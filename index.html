Argentina (Spanish pronunciation: [aɾxentina]), officially the Argentine Republic (Spanish: República Argentina), is a country in the southern half of South America. Argentina covers an area of 2,780,400 km2 (1,073,500 sq mi),

Argentina is the second largest country in South America behind Brazil. The country has a detailed history and many tourist attractions that make it an attractive place to visit.
